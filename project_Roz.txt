 Based on photographic data of the Rozhen National Astronomical 
 Observatory, Institute of Astronomy, Bulgarian Academy of Sciences 
 obtained using the 50/70 cm Schmidt telescope. The plates were 
 digitized using professional flatbed scanners EPSON 10000XL/V700 
 and processed in the present digital form. The project of Plate 
 digitization was funded by the grants of Bulgarian Ministry of 
 Education and Science DO-02-273/275,18.12.2009. 
 
 Investigators using these scans are kindly requested to include the 
 above acknowledgements in any publications.
