 Based on photographic data of the Bucharest Observatory of the 
 Insititute of Astronomy Romaniam Academy obtained using the 16 cm
 Zeiss camera and 38 cm Prin-Merz reflector. The plates were
 digitized using professional flatbed scanner UMAX Alpha Vista II and
 processed in the present digital form. The project of plate 
 digitization was supported in the frames of the bilateral cooperation
 between Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and Romanian Academy and 
 partially by the grants of the Bulgarian Ministry of Education and 
 Science DO-02-273/275,18.12.2009. 
 
 The investigators using these scans are kindly requested to include
 the above acknowledgements in any publications.
