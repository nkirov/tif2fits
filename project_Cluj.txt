 Based on photographic data of the Cluj Observatory of the Insatitute
 of Astronomy Romaniam Acadely obtained using the 20/300 cm 
 Zeiss Astrograf and 50/250 cm Zeiss Reflector.The plates were 
 digitized using professional flatbed scanner EPSON V750 PRO and 
 processed in the present digital form. The project of plate 
 digitization was funded in  the frames of the bilateral cooperation  
 between Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and Romaian Academy and 
 partially by the grants of the Bulgarian Ministry of Education and 
 Science DO-02-273/275,18.12.2009. 
 
 The investigators using these scans are kindly requested to include
 the above acknowledgements in any publications.
